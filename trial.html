Card Damage

How to Use

Play for free with your friend from anywhere in the world!

Click a card in hand to select it, then click a slot to move it

Click a card in lineup to select it, then click scrapheap to move it

Click the forge to draw a card

Click an energy counter to toggle it

Doubleclick a card in lineup to edit its damage

Click the toggle hand to toggle display of your hand

Open a new tab with the ignite roller and sharescreens to play your friend(s) remotely!

Remember to turn display fo your hand off when sharing, and pause sharing to summon minions
