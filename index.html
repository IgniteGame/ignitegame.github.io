ABOUT

Ignite is a quick and simple board game where players race to destroy each other's forges by timing the ignition of their bots within a battlefield of crazy combos and epic explosions.

Command an army of bots and forgelings to destroy and take over your opponent’s forge before they destroy yours! In Ignos, magical forges leftover from wars past are scattered around the island. Use your forge’s energy to summon minions to attack and defend for you and bots to ignite for you. Set up your forgeling to tinker with your bots to produce insane combos. You'll need your forgeling’s mechanical skill and the luck of random ignition to destroy and conquer your opponent’s forge in this quick action packed game.

GAMEPLAY

Players gain energy each turn to summon minions from their hand. By carefully planning when you summon and attack with your minions you can create combos to maximum the number of explosions, occurring right at the perfect time. Explosions can hit your opponent’s minions or their forge. When a player runs out of cards in their forge, they lose.

Each player is equipped with a 30 card forge full of minions. All minions have an energy cost, attack and health, shown by lightning, wrench, and gear symbols respectively. "Bot" minions also have three ignite values shown in flaming stones. When you IGNITE, you roll two six-sided dice, and one determines which target you hit and the other how much damage you deal, which corresponds to the ignite values on the card. When minions attack, they deal damage equal to their attack value, and when they take damage, if they have no health they are destroyed. When a player takes damage they scrap that many cards, which means place that many cards from their forge into their scrapheap. The scrapheap is also where dead minions go. Almost all of the deck is either bots that ignite, or forgeling (curious, evil gremlin creatures) that interact with bots and their ignite abilities. By attacking with your minions and igniting your bots, you can destroy your opponent's minions or destroy their forge and win the game!
