ABOUT

IGNITE is a fast-paced game where you race to destroy opposing forges by commanding your minions to ignite, attack, and combo your way to victory!

Become a Forgemaster and command an army of bots and forgelings and destroy your opponent’s forge before they destroy yours! In Ignos, magical forges leftover from wars past are scattered around the land. Use your forge’s energy to summon minions to attack, defend, and ignite for you. Set up your forgeling to tinker with your bots to produce insane combos. You'll need your forgelings' mechanical skills and the luck of random ignitions to destroy and conquer your opponent’s forge in this quick, action packed game.

GAMEPLAY

Players gain energy each turn to summon minions from their hand. Ignites can hit your opponent’s minions or their forge . When a player runs out of cards in their forge , they lose.

Each player is equipped with a 30 card forge full of minions. All minions have an energy cost , attack and health , shown by lightning, wrench, and gear symbols respectively. Bots also have three ignite values shown in flaming stones.

When you IGNITE, you roll two six-sided dice (try it here). One die determines which target you hit and the other how much damage you deal, corresponding to the ignite values on the card. When minions attack, they deal damage equal to their attack value, can take damage equal to their health until they are destroyed. Minion's also have their attack reduced by the amount of damage they have taken.

When a player takes damage they remove that many cards from their forge and place them into their scrapheap . The scrapheap is also where destroyed minions go. Your forge is filled with bots that ignite and forgelings (curious, evil gremlin creatures) that love tinkering with your bots. By attacking and igniting, you can destroy your opponent's minions and destroy their forge and win the game!

Players start the game with 5 cards, and start their turns with 4 energy . There are two parts of a turn: attacking and summoning. During attacking, players can attack any target with one exception: that player's Forge Guardian (minion on slot 1) must be attacked and destroyed first. During summoning, that player can spend energy to draw cards (1 energy per card) or summon minions. The first turn of the game starts with only 2 energy.

Ignite scales great with more players! Players simply sit in a circle, can only attack adjacent players, and ignites hit BOTH players! Get ready for some chaotic fun!
